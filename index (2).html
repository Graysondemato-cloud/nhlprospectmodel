What the model has learned
Skating: most reliably transferable skillAcceleration + edgework convert at 2.4× the rate of static skills. Every franchise-tier player in the database has skating averaging 8.5+.
Playmaking vision: best predictor of stardomCrosby (10), Hughes (10), Makar (10), MacKinnon (9) — not one franchise player has vision below 9. Most heavily weighted skill for forwards.
Compete/motor: rescues undersized playersKonecny (5-10/175), Jarvis (5-10/179), Stankoven (5-8/168) all overcame size with elite compete + skating. Model does NOT penalize small players with elite motor.
Size without skating = strongest bust predictorBig players with skating below 6.0 fail at 70%+ in the database. Yakupov + Strome both fit this pattern combined with low IQ.
Decision speed: the AHL trapMost common reason prospects stall. Time/space compresses dramatically at NHL level. Hard to develop post-draft. Low marks = career ceiling, not development area.
